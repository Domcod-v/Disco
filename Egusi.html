How I Make vegetable Soup

Ingrediants for egusi soup

  • Ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • Palm oil
  • Beef, goat meat, or chicken
  • Stockfish or dry fish
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Pepper (fresh or blended)
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Ogiri or iru (optional for flavor)
  • Leafy vegetables (ugu/pumpkin leaves, spinach, or bitterleaf)
  • Water or meat stock

    • How to prepare Egusi soup

    Boil the meat
    Put the meat,onoins,Seasoning salt and water in a pot,cook until the meat is soft,add Stockfish if you are using it
    Prepare the egusi paste
    Mix the grounded egusi with a little water to make a thick paste
    Add papper and crayfish
    Pour in blended pepper and grounded crayfish.let it cook for about 5 minutes
    Add the egusi
    Scoop the egusi paste into the soup or pour it in and stir.Allow it to cook for about 10-15 minutes
    Add vegetables
    Put in ugu,spinach or bitterleaf
    • Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes
    • Add salt and Seasoning if needed and then turn off heat
    Best eaten with:
    Garri (eba)
    Pounded yam
    Fufu
    Semovita

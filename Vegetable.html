How I Make Egusi Soup

Ingrediants used in cooking vegetable soup

  • Fresh snails
  • Pumpkin leaves (Ugu) or waterleaf
  • Palm oil
  • Pepper (fresh or dry)
  • Onions
  • Crayfish (ground)
  • Stockfish or dry fish (optional)
  • Meat or pomo (optional)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Periwinkle (optional)
  • Garlic or ginger (optional)
  • Water or meat stock

How To Cook Vegetable Soup

Clean the snails
Wash the snails very well with salt, lime, or alum to remove slime. Rinse many times.
Boil the snails
Put the snails in a pot with a little salt, onion, and seasoning. Cook for about 10–15 minutes so they become soft.
Prepare the vegetables
Wash the ugu and waterleaf. Slice them into small pieces.
Cook the proteins
If you are using meat, stockfish, or dry fish, cook them first with seasoning, onion, and a little water until soft.
Add palm oil
Pour palm oil into the pot of cooked meat/snail. Add crayfish and pepper Add ground crayfish, pepper, and seasoning cubes. Stir and allow it cook for a few minutes.
Add waterleaf first
Waterleaf produces water, so cook it for about 3–5 minutes.
Add ugu leaves
Stir and allow to cook for another 2–3 minutes. Do not overcook so the vegetables stay green.
  • Taste and adjust
  • Add salt or seasoning if needed.
  • Bring it down,once the vegetables are soft and everything is well mixed, your soup is ready.

Best eaten with:

Garri (eba)
Pounded yam
Fufu
Semovita
